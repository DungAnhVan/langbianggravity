Brand position

Not a generic spare-parts shop.

Langbiang Gravity is an engineering-first off-road brand. Product choices start with fitment, material behavior, tooth wear, chain line, heat treatment, and repeated dirt testing, then move into controlled small-batch manufacturing and selected official garage supply.

The goal is simple: parts that feel premium on the bench, survive rental-fleet abuse, and make mechanical sense for Vietnam riding while staying export-ready for Japan, Australia, New Zealand, and global off-road customers.
